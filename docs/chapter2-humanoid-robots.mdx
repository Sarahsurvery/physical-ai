Humanoid robots are robots designed to resemble the human body in shape and movement. They usually have a head, arms, torso, and legs, allowing them to operate in environments built for humans.

The main purpose of humanoid robots is:

To work alongside humans

To perform tasks in human-centered spaces

To communicate naturally with people

Applications of humanoid robots include:

Healthcare assistance

Education and tutoring

Customer service

Research and experimentation

Humanoid robots use Physical AI to balance, walk, recognize faces, understand speech, and respond intelligently. Famous examples include robots used for research, caregiving, and social interaction.
