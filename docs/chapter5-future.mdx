Human-Robot Interaction (HRI) focuses on how humans and robots communicate and collaborate.

In the future, robots will:

Understand human emotions

Communicate using natural language

Adapt to individual human needs

Work safely in shared environments

Future developments include:

Social robots for companionship

Assistive robots for elderly care

Collaborative robots (cobots) in industries

Intelligent humanoids for daily tasks

Ethical considerations such as safety, privacy, and trust will play a critical role in shaping the future of Physical AI and humanoid robotics.
